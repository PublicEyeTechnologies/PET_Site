Who are we?

Public Eye Technologies Ltd, was founded in the UK to research and develop innovative solutions that automate manual tasks.

Our team have relevant and modern expertise in Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Computer Science, which provides an innovative environment where rapid application development and prototyping can flourish.

Innovation

One of our new developments in progress is moddid.com, a unique solution to log, monitor, and analyse the temperature and relative humidity for industrial and commercial grade environments. Our online panel provides an intuitive way to customise moddid.com to desired operational specifications and visualise recorded values. This includes a real-time alarming facility providing email and text-based alerts. This ensures you are always aware of significant temperature and relative humidity changes before they become a problem.
